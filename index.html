Investment Focus

Cognum is a privately held investment company focused on scalable digital products, platforms, and services. Our core strength lies in business models that combine technology with strong customer, partner, or user engagement.

We are industry-agnostic, but consistently drawn to platform and service business models, enterprise software, and creative industries.

Norway is our home market—and a global leader in digitalization. We also explore emerging opportunities in Africa and select European markets. Current areas of special interest include hardware-enabled services and space.

What we look for above all is an exceptional founding team.

What We Bring

We contribute to growth through:

  • Scaling strategy and customer focus
  • Product engagement and process improvement
  • Business model development and go-to-market refinement

We are usually passive, but occasionally take hands-on roles in companies where we can create disproportionate value.
